Eva Myra May is a multi-instrumentalist, songwriter, producer, and DJ specializing in synthesis and sound design. Her work entails a hybrid of genres and techniques stemming from her vast list of influences and never-ending interest in technology. Her creations are deeply personal and centered around one thing; what the tools can help us feel.

Eva can be heard all around Los Angeles, at venues and parties including The Roxy, The Echo / Echoplex, The Resident, Zebulon, NonPlus Ultra, The Peppermint Club, SoHo House, The Lash, Chardogay, Queerspace, Ostbahnhof, as well as a 1.5 year long residency DJing for Jolene, held monthly at Precinct in DTLA.
